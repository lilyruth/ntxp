The vitex shrub is deciduous and can grow up to 15 feet tall. When in bloom, it has an enchanting fragrance. Vitex is drought tolerant.

Although beautiful, many roses are prone to Rose Rosette Disease. The Top Gun variety is more resistant than other roses.

Boxwood shrubs don't get enough love. They can grow in almost any condition. They can be shaped and serve as an uncomplaining evergreen backdrop for flowers.

Privet is an evergreen, versatile shrub with multiple popular varieties, including Sunshine, which is a bright gold color, and variegated. Privet can be happy in many places, including in pots or as a hedge.

In early spring, quince will bloom before the leaves grow. The pop of color is enchanting. Many quince varieties even produce an edible fruit!
