The Creek Willow is a fast growing tree and easy to propagate. It provides shade and is an Earth Kind tree. It does have additional watering needs.

The Crape Myrtle is a hardy tree, especially once established. Its striking blooms flower throughout the summer.

An evergreen favorite, the magnolia tree produces large, fragrant blooms.

The redbud tree is known for its burst of color in early spring.

The Nellie Stevens Holly is a beautiful, hardy breed, growing upwards of 25 feet.
